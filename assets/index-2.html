  • Chairman's Message

ITI Hansi is one of the most prestigious and renowned Industrial Training institute of North India. The aim of the institute is to provide technically skilled manpower to industries by providing training in a safe 'hands on' workshop style environment.The endeavour is also to reduce unemployment among educated youth by equipping them for suitable industrial employment- which, from a macro-perspective, is an important step in the direction of utilizing country's demographic dividend.It's qualified trainers who have up-to-date knowledge and experience of industry are dedicated to help students, realize their complete potential who can adjust to changing demands of industry as well as be future entrepreneurs.
